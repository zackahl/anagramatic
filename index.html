Rules
  • You have 1 minute to to write as many words as you can.
  • Words must contain 3 or more letters.
  • Words must only contain the letters from the current word.
  • You cannot use the same letter multiple times unless it appears more than once in the given word.
  • 3-8 letter words score 0.5, 1, 2, 3, 4, 5 points respectively.
  • Anagrams score a 10 × multiplier e.g. 4 letter anagram = 10 points.
Shortcut Keys
  • Press enter to check the word.
  • Press spacebar to change the word.
